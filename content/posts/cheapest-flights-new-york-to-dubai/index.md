## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

![cheapest-flights-new-york-to-dubai](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-new-york-to-dubai/body_3.jpg)


Choosing between direct and connecting flights can affect both your budget and travel experience. Direct flights from New York to Dubai, such as those offered by Emirates for $663, provide the convenience of a non-stop journey, typically lasting around 14 hours. This option is ideal for travelers who prefer to minimize travel time and avoid the hassle of layovers. On the other hand, connecting flights, like those from Saudia, offer lower fares starting at $546. However, these flights may involve longer travel times and additional layovers, which can add to the overall journey duration. Ultimately, the choice between direct and connecting flights will depend on your priorities, whether they be cost savings or convenience.
