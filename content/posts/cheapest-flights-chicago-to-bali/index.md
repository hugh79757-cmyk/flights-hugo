When planning a trip to Bali, timing can significantly influence the cost of your flight. Generally, booking flights about two to three months in advance can yield better prices. The peak season in Bali usually falls between June and August, as well as during the December holidays, when prices are often at their highest. Conversely, traveling during the shoulder seasons, which are April to June and September to October, can provide more affordable options. Additionally, being flexible with your travel dates can help you take advantage of lower fares.

## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

![cheapest-flights-chicago-to-bali](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-chicago-to-bali/body_3.jpg)


When considering flights from Chicago to Bali, travelers have the option of direct or connecting flights. Direct flights are typically more convenient, allowing you to reach your destination without layovers. However, they may come at a premium price. Connecting flights, while potentially cheaper, can add significant travel time due to layovers, which may be in other cities or countries. It’s essential to weigh the trade-offs between time and cost when making your decision. If you have the flexibility, exploring both options can lead to significant savings.
