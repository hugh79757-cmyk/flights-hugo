## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

![cheapest-flights-los-angeles-to-prague](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-los-angeles-to-prague/body_3.jpg)


Choosing between direct and connecting flights can affect both your travel experience and your budget. Direct flights from Los Angeles to Prague are often more convenient, allowing you to arrive at your destination without the hassle of layovers. However, they may come at a premium price. On the other hand, connecting flights might offer more competitive rates, but they can extend your travel time significantly. If you’re willing to spend a few extra hours in transit, opting for a connecting flight could save you money.
