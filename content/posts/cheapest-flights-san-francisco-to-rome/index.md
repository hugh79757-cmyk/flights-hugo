## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

Choosing between direct and connecting flights can significantly affect your travel experience and budget. Direct flights, such as those offered by ITA Airways, provide the convenience of a non-stop journey from San Francisco to Rome, which can be particularly appealing if you prefer a hassle-free travel experience. However, these flights are currently priced higher at $968. On the other hand, connecting flights, like those from Turkish Airlines, can offer substantial savings, with fares starting at $678 for one-stop itineraries. While these flights may take longer due to the layover, they can be a smart choice for budget-conscious travelers.

## What to Expect When You Arrive in Rome

![cheapest-flights-san-francisco-to-rome](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-san-francisco-to-rome/body_6.jpg)


Arriving in Rome is an exciting experience filled with long history. The city’s main airport, Leonardo da Vinci International Airport (Fiumicino), is well-connected to the city center via various transportation options, including trains, buses, and taxis. Expect to navigate through customs and immigration upon arrival, which can take some time depending on the number of flights landing simultaneously. Once you’re in the city, you’ll find a wealth of attractions, from ancient ruins like the Colosseum to charming neighborhoods such as Trastevere. Be prepared for a mix of modern conveniences and historic charm as you explore the Italian capital.

By keeping these insights in mind, you can make informed decisions about your travel from San Francisco to Rome, ensuring a smooth and budget-friendly journey.
