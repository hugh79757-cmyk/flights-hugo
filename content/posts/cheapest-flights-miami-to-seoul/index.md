This window allows you to take advantage of lower fares before prices begin to rise as the departure date approaches. Additionally, consider traveling during the shoulder seasons, which are spring (April to June) and fall (September to November). ## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

![cheapest-flights-miami-to-seoul](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-miami-to-seoul/body_3.jpg)


Choosing between direct and connecting flights can significantly impact your travel experience. Direct flights from Miami to Seoul are typically more convenient, allowing you to reach your destination without layovers. However, they may also come at a premium price. Connecting flights, on the other hand, can offer substantial savings, although they may extend your travel time. If you’re willing to spend extra hours in transit, connecting flights can often provide more budget-friendly options.
