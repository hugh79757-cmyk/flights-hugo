## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

![cheapest-flights-san-francisco-to-mexico-city](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-san-francisco-to-mexico-city/body_3.jpg)


Choosing between direct and connecting flights can significantly impact your travel experience. Direct flights, like those offered by Aeromexico, provide the convenience of reaching your destination without layovers, which can save time and reduce travel fatigue. While connecting flights may sometimes be cheaper, they often involve longer travel times and the potential for missed connections. For those looking to maximize their time in Mexico City, opting for a direct flight is usually the more efficient choice.
