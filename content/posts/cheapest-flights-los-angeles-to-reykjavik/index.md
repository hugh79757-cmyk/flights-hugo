## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

![cheapest-flights-los-angeles-to-reykjavik](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-los-angeles-to-reykjavik/body_3.jpg)


When considering flights from Los Angeles to Reykjavik, travelers have the option of direct or connecting flights. Direct flights, while often more expensive, can save you time and hassle, as they eliminate layovers. However, connecting flights may offer lower fares and can be a good choice for those willing to spend a bit more time traveling. Depending on the airline and the specific route, connecting flights may have layovers in cities such as [New York](https://michelin.techpawz.com/posts/michelin-restaurants-new-york/) , [London](https://michelin.techpawz.com/posts/michelin-restaurants-london/) , or [Seattle](https://deals.techpawz.com/posts/flight-deals-from-seattle/) , which could also provide an opportunity to explore another destination along the way.
