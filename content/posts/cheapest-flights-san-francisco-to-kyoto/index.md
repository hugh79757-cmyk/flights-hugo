## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

![cheapest-flights-san-francisco-to-kyoto](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-san-francisco-to-kyoto/body_3.jpg)


Travelers from San Francisco to Kyoto can choose between direct and connecting flights. Direct flights offer the convenience of a non-stop journey, which can save time and reduce travel fatigue. However, connecting flights can sometimes be more economical, especially if you are flexible with your travel schedule. While direct flights may be limited, connecting flights often provide more options and can include layovers in major hubs such as [Los Angeles](https://michelin.techpawz.com/posts/michelin-restaurants-los-angeles/) , Tokyo, or Seattle. When weighing your options, consider both the total travel time and the cost, as well as any potential layover durations.
