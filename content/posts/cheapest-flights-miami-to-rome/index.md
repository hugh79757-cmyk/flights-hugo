## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

![cheapest-flights-miami-to-rome](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-miami-to-rome/body_3.jpg)


Choosing between direct and connecting flights can influence both your travel experience and your budget. Direct flights, like those offered by ITA Airways, provide convenience and save time, as they eliminate the need for layovers. However, they often come at a premium, with current prices at $593. On the other hand, connecting flights, such as those offered by Air Europa, can be more economical. Although they may extend your travel time due to layovers, they can save you money. The choice ultimately depends on your priorities—whether you value time or cost savings more.
