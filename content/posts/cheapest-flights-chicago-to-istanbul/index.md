## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

![cheapest-flights-chicago-to-istanbul](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-chicago-to-istanbul/body_3.jpg)


Choosing between direct and connecting flights often depends on personal preference and budget considerations. Direct flights provide the convenience of a non-stop journey, reducing travel time considerably. Turkish Airlines offers direct flights from Chicago to Istanbul, which is ideal for those who prefer a quicker and more straightforward travel experience.

On the other hand, connecting flights, such as those offered by Etihad Airways and KLM, can be more economical. For instance, the lowest price for a one-stop flight is $620 with Etihad Airways, while KLM offers similar itineraries for $705. While these flights may take longer due to layovers, they can be a great option for budget-conscious travelers.
