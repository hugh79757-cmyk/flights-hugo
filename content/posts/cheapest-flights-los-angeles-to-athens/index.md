## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

![cheapest-flights-los-angeles-to-athens](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-los-angeles-to-athens/body_3.jpg)


When considering flights from Los Angeles to Athens, travelers have the option of choosing between direct and connecting flights. Direct flights are typically more convenient, allowing you to arrive in Athens without the hassle of layovers. However, they may also come at a premium price. Connecting flights, while potentially cheaper, can add several hours to your travel time, depending on the length of the layover and the connecting airport. If you’re looking to save money, a connecting flight might be the better option, but if you prioritize time and convenience, a direct flight could be worth the extra cost.
