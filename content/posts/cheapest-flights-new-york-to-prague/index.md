## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

![cheapest-flights-new-york-to-prague](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-new-york-to-prague/body_3.jpg)


Choosing between direct and connecting flights can affect not only your travel time but also your overall experience. Direct flights, such as those offered by KLM from New York to Prague, provide a straightforward travel option without the hassle of layovers. This can be particularly appealing for travelers looking to minimize travel time and maximize their time in Prague. Connecting flights may offer lower prices, but they can also lead to longer travel times and increased stress due to layover durations. For those who prioritize convenience, direct flights are often the preferred choice.
