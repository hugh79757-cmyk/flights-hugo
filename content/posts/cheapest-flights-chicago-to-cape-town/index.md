## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

![cheapest-flights-chicago-to-cape-town](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-chicago-to-cape-town/body_3.jpg)


When considering flights from Chicago to Cape Town, travelers often weigh the advantages of direct versus connecting flights. Direct flights are typically more convenient, as they eliminate the need for layovers and can save you several hours of travel time. However, direct flights may be less common for this route, and prices can be higher. Connecting flights, while potentially longer, often come with lower fares and more options for scheduling. If you choose a connecting flight, be sure to account for layover times and the possibility of flight delays.
