## Current Flight Prices: Miami to Singapore

When considering flights from Miami (MIA) to Singapore (SIN) in 2026, the cheapest month to fly is June, with prices starting at $1589 for a round trip on American Airlines, which involves two stops. If you’re planning to travel later in the year, December flights are available for $1796 on TAP [Portugal](https://visafree.techpawz.com/posts/portugal-visa-free/) , also with two stops. Notably, this price holds steady for several flights on December 20, indicating that this date is particularly popular for travelers heading to Singapore.
