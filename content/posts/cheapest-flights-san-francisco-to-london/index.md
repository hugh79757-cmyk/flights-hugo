## Best Time to Book Flights to London

![cheapest-flights-san-francisco-to-london](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-san-francisco-to-london/body_2.jpg)


Timing is everything when it comes to booking flights. Generally, travelers should aim to book their flights at least two to three months in advance to secure the best deals. For flights from San Francisco to London, the cheapest fares can typically be found during the late spring to early summer months, particularly in May and August. 

For instance, if you are looking to fly in August, booking early could land you a fare as low as $560.00. In contrast, waiting until the last minute could result in higher prices, with fares rising significantly as departure dates approach. 

Additionally, consider flying mid-week, as Tuesday and Wednesday flights often yield lower prices compared to weekend departures.

## Money-Saving Tips for San Francisco to London Flights

![cheapest-flights-san-francisco-to-london](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-san-francisco-to-london/body_5.jpg)


Traveling on a budget doesn’t mean you have to sacrifice comfort or convenience. Here are some practical tips to help you save money on flights from San Francisco to London:

1. **Be Flexible with Your Travel Dates**: If your travel schedule allows for flexibility, use fare comparison tools to find the cheapest days to fly. Prices can vary significantly depending on the day of the week and time of year.

2. **Set Fare Alerts**: Many travel websites and apps allow you to set alerts for specific routes. This way, you’ll be notified when prices drop, enabling you to book at the right time.

3. **Consider Nearby Airports**: While San Francisco International Airport (SFO) is the primary departure point, also check flights from nearby airports like Oakland (OAK) or San Jose (SJC). Sometimes, flying from a different airport can lead to substantial savings.

4. **Book Early**: As previously mentioned, booking your flight several months in advance can lead to significant savings. Keep an eye on prices and book when you see a good deal.

5. **Use Frequent Flyer Miles**: If you have accumulated miles with an airline, consider using them to offset the cost of your ticket. This can make a significant difference in your overall travel expenses.

## What to Expect When You Arrive in London

![cheapest-flights-san-francisco-to-london](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-san-francisco-to-london/body_6.jpg)


Arriving in London is always a thrilling experience. The city is a bustling metropolis, rich in history and culture. Upon landing, you can expect to go through customs and immigration, which can take some time, especially during peak travel seasons.

Once you clear customs, you’ll find various transportation options to reach your destination. The London Underground (Tube) is efficient and connects you to many parts of the city. Alternatively, you can take a taxi or use rideshare services like Uber for a more direct route to your accommodation.

As you explore London, don’t miss iconic landmarks such as the Tower of London, Buckingham Palace, and the British Museum. The city also boasts a vibrant food scene, with options ranging from traditional British fare to international cuisine.
