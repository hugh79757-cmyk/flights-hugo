## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

When considering your options for flights from San Francisco to Athens, you will encounter both direct and connecting flights. Direct flights, while more convenient, are often more expensive and may not be available every day. Connecting flights can offer more flexibility in terms of price and scheduling, but they will extend your travel time. If you’re looking to save money, a connecting flight may be the better choice, especially if you’re willing to have a longer travel experience.
