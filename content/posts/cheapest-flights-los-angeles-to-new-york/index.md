When planning your trip from Los Angeles to New York, timing is crucial for securing the best flight deals. Generally, it's advisable to book your flights at least three to four weeks in advance. This window allows you to take advantage of lower fares before prices begin to rise closer to the departure date.

Historically, flights to New York tend to be cheaper during the shoulder seasons, which include late winter and early spring (January to March) and late fall (September to November). However, since you are looking to fly in May, you should consider booking as soon as possible, especially since May is a popular month for travel due to pleasant weather and various events in New York.

Currently, Spirit Airlines offers a direct flight from Los Angeles to New York for $215.00, which is a reasonable price considering the time saved.

On the other hand, connecting flights can often be more economical. For instance, the lowest fare of $165.00 via Trip.com for a one-stop flight on May 1, 2026, is significantly cheaper than the direct option. Ultimately, the choice between direct and connecting flights will depend on your budget, schedule, and personal preferences. If time is of the essence, a direct flight might be worth the extra cost. But if you're looking to save money and don't mind a longer travel time, a connecting flight could be the way to go.

## Money-Saving Tips for Los Angeles to New York Flights

![cheapest-flights-los-angeles-to-new-york](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-los-angeles-to-new-york/body_5.jpg)


Traveling from Los Angeles to New York doesn't have to break the bank. Here are some practical money-saving tips to help you secure the best flight deals:

1. **Be Flexible with Your Travel Dates**: If your schedule allows, consider flying on weekdays rather than weekends, as fares are often lower during the week. 

2. **Book in Advance**: As mentioned earlier, booking your flights at least three to four weeks ahead of your intended travel date can help you snag lower fares.

3. **Use Fare Comparison Websites**: Utilize travel search engines to compare prices across different airlines and booking platforms. This can help you identify the best deals available.

4. **Sign Up for Alerts**: Many travel websites offer fare alerts that notify you when prices drop for your desired route. This can be a great way to catch deals before they disappear.

5. **Consider Nearby Airports**: If you're willing to travel a bit further, check for flights departing from or arriving at nearby airports. Sometimes, flying into Newark (EWR) or LaGuardia (LGA) can be cheaper than flying directly into JFK.

6. **Travel Light**: If possible, avoid checked baggage fees by traveling with just a carry-on. This can save you money, especially on low-cost carriers.
