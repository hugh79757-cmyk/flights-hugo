## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

![cheapest-flights-san-francisco-to-hanoi](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-san-francisco-to-hanoi/body_3.jpg)


When choosing between direct and connecting flights from San Francisco to Hanoi, there are a few factors to consider. Direct flights are often more convenient, allowing you to reach your destination without layovers. However, they may come at a premium price. Connecting flights, while potentially cheaper, can add several hours to your travel time, so weighing the cost against convenience is important. If you have flexibility in your travel dates, searching for both options can help you find the best deal.
