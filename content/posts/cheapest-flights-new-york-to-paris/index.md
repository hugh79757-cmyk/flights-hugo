To maximize your travel budget when booking flights from New York to Paris, consider the following strategies. First, be flexible with your travel dates. The city is known for its cafes, boutiques, and art, offering a unique experience for every visitor. With a well-planned itinerary, you can make the most of your time in this historic city.
