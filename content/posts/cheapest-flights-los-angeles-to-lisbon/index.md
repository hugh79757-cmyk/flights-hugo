Choosing between direct and connecting flights often depends on personal preferences and budget. Direct flights, like those offered by TAP Portugal, provide a seamless travel experience, taking you straight from Los Angeles to Lisbon without the hassle of layovers. If you value convenience and time efficiency, opting for a direct flight might be the better choice, especially when considering the current prices.
