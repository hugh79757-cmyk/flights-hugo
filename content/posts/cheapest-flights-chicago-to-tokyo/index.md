## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

When deciding between direct and connecting flights, travelers should consider both convenience and cost. Direct flights, such as those offered by All Nippon Airways at $908, provide a straightforward travel experience without the hassle of layovers. However, connecting flights can often present more budget-friendly options. For instance, Frontier Airlines offers flights with two stops for $822, and Spirit Airlines provides a one-stop option for $785 in August. While connecting flights may extend travel time, they can significantly reduce overall costs, making them an attractive choice for those willing to trade convenience for savings.
