## Best Time to Book Flights to Lisbon

![cheapest-flights-chicago-to-lisbon](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-chicago-to-lisbon/body_2.jpg)


When planning a trip to Lisbon, timing your flight purchase can significantly influence the fare you pay. Generally, booking your flights around two to three months in advance can yield the best prices. For travel during peak seasons, such as summer, it’s advisable to book even earlier, as prices tend to rise as the departure date approaches. Additionally, consider flying mid-week, as flights on Tuesdays and Wednesdays often come at lower prices compared to weekends.

## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

![cheapest-flights-chicago-to-lisbon](https://pub-2f5c7af1c303419a933069212bc25874.r2.dev/etap/cheapest-flights-chicago-to-lisbon/body_3.jpg)


Choosing between direct and connecting flights can impact both your travel experience and your budget. Direct flights, like those available from Chicago to Lisbon via TAP Portugal, offer the convenience of reaching your destination without layovers, which can save time and reduce the stress associated with changing planes. While connecting flights may be cheaper in some cases, they can add hours to your travel time and increase the risk of delays. In this instance, with direct flights priced at $784, you may find that the convenience justifies the cost.

## Money-Saving Tips for Chicago to Lisbon Flights

To maximize your savings when flying from Chicago to Lisbon, consider the following strategies. First, sign up for fare alerts from various travel websites and airlines to stay informed about price drops. Flexibility with your travel dates can also lead to significant savings; being willing to adjust your departure or return dates by a few days can make a difference in fare prices. Additionally, consider flying during the shoulder seasons, which typically see lower demand and more competitive pricing. Finally, always compare prices across multiple platforms to ensure you’re getting the best deal available.
