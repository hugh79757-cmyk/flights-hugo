## Direct vs. Connecting Flights

Choosing between direct and connecting flights can impact both your travel experience and costs. Direct flights, like those offered by TAP Portugal, provide a straightforward travel experience with no layovers, reducing overall travel time. While connecting flights may present lower fares, they often come with longer travel durations and potential delays. If convenience and time are your priorities, opting for a direct flight may be worth the additional cost.
